Introduction

I am a college freshman from Wilmington Delaware studying business marketing. Growing up I never used much technology because I was outside for the majority of my time. However, as I got older I started using technology to play video games with my siblings which became a way for us to bond. Then when I got to elementary school a lot of my classmates had phones and I didn't but my parents bought me an ipod touch which was not the same but similar enough. Finally, in middle school I got an iphone and used it all the time, especially social media which I still use in college. When deciding on where to go to high school I considered majoring in communication arts where I would have to know how to manipulate photos through photoshop and adobe. With some coaching I was able to do some photo editing through adobe but that was many years ago and my skills are not as good as they once were. In college I am taking a class to learn how to program to improve my skills in business.
